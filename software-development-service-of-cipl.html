Software Development and Maintenance

At CIPL, we believe in developing a software fitting to the requirements of our client as different clients may have different requirements. We are committed to offer complete satisfaction and value for money to our clients but also helping our clients in better and efficient use of software solutions. We believe in collecting valuable feed back of our clients. It helps us in analyzing our services and guides for a continuous improvement to achieve the highest standard of quality.

We provide both end-to-end offshore software development solutions - from requirements gathering to application design and development to application maintenance - and specific design and development expertise. We're experts in various software platforms such as Visual Studio (.NET), Visual Age (Java Development tool), etc. Our teams employ industry standard tools, such as Rational Rose and Designer 2000, and techniques during the software development lifecycle.
